Engineering Student  •  Robotics Creator  •  3D Visualizer  •  Music Collaborator

Welcome to my digital space. I am focused on the intersection of hardware, structural design, and creative arts. I build robots, conceptualize complex models in 3D/CAD environments, and dive deep into musical collaborations. I wire circuits and design physical components — bringing absolute functionality to creative visions.

(Not a web or software developer; my language is exact measurements, servos, metal, logic boards, and sound waves).
